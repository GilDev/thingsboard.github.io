In the world of IoT platforms, ThingsBoard stands out with its flexible offerings tailored to various needs and budgets. Today, we’re diving into the different flavors of ThingsBoard to help you pick the one that fits your project like a glove. Let’s break it down, shall we?<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Community Edition (CE)<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Starting with the Community Edition it’s the open-source hero of the lineup. The CE is totally free and offers a solid foundation with limited features. You’re looking at basic asset management, real-time dashboards, and customizable goodies. It’s a DIY enthusiast’s dream, with system admin access and the freedom to play around with the code. Check out the live demo here<\/a> before you dive in.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Professional Edition (PE) Self-Managed<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Next up, the Professional Edition steps it up a notch. It’s the premium, closed-source variant for those who mean business. Unlimited features? Check. Various integration options out-of-the-box? You got it. This edition is self-managed, meaning you’re in charge of deployment and management, but with the added perks of advanced rule engine, entity groups, and white-labeling options. For the power users out there, it’s worth every penny. Check out the pricing options here<\/a>. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

Professional Edition Cloud (PE PaaS)<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Last but not least, the PE Cloud version takes the hassle out of hosting. It’s the same closed-source, feature-packed PE but managed by the ThingsBoard team. You get everything from the self-managed PE plus the convenience of cloud hosting. It’s subscription-based and covers hosting costs, making it a breeze to get started. Fancy a trial? Sign up here<\/a> and take it for a spin.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
